I am an MSE student in Computer and Information Sciences at the University of Pennsylvania, concentrating in AI/ML. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, multimodal large language models, applied mathematics, and production software engineering.

I am currently a Software Engineer Intern at Amazon and a Graduate Research Assistant with Penn Engineering AI, where I research multimodal LLMs. Previously, I worked as an Undergraduate Research Assistant at Wake Forest University on interdisciplinary machine learning, applied math, and biology research, and as a Machine Learning Engineer Intern at PARATERA.
